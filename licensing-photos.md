Nearly all of the images on this site are my own. Any photos taken by other people are credited as such and used with permission.

Some of my images are available for non-exclusive licensing on a case-by-case basis. Rates start at $150 USD. {% include email.html text="Contact me" %} for a quote.[^fn1]

Please include the following information with your inquiry:

- Which images you are interested in licensing.
- How the images are going to be used.
- The size (dimensions) the images will be.
- If the images will be printed, the size (quantity) of the print run.

If any of my images are used without prior permission, the after-the-fact bill is tripled to a minimum of $450.
